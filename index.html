About Us – Root Marks

Root Marks is more than just a brand — it's a vision that took root in the heart of Nived Devan, who founded it during his teenage years with a mission to build something meaningful, helpful, and rooted in strong community values.

What began as a small travel initiative has now evolved into a dynamic multi-service platform with the help of his friends and mentors offering professional services in tours and travels, event management, and real estate. At its core, Root Marks is driven by a commitment to quality, affordability, and the empowerment of local communities.

We aim to make travel more accessible through our branch Tripy Kerala, offering customizable, budget-friendly and luxurious travel packages that help you truly explore the culture, beauty, and heritage of each destination.

In the events space, we organize everything from destination weddings and birthdays to musical shows and public functions, handling all aspects — from venue and décor to entertainment and logistics.

Our real estate division connects people with opportunities to buy, sell, or invest in properties with transparency and trust.

At Root Marks, we don’t just offer services — we build experiences, opportunities, and a community that grows together.

Our Motive – Root Marks

  • Affordable Services for Everyone: We aim to offer high-quality services at the lowest possible cost, making travel, events, and property solutions accessible to all, regardless of budget.
  • Empowering Youth through Part-Time Jobs: We actively create part-time job opportunities for students and others, helping them earn, learn, and grow within the fields of tourism, event management, and real estate.
  • Redefining the Meaning of Tourism: We strive to show people the true essence of tourism — not just visiting places, but understanding the culture, connecting with nature, and experiencing places with purpose and respect.
  • Creating Meaningful Experiences: Every service we offer — whether it's a trip, a celebration, or a home — is designed to create memories, build connections, and leave a positive mark on the lives of our clients.
  • Supporting Local Communities: We prioritize local collaborations, support small businesses, and aim to boost the local economy through tourism and real estate engagement.
  • Building a Community of Shared Values: We aim to build a community of people who believe in growth, service, culture, and connection — a network that supports one another and grows together with shared purpose.
